What to do in Nürnberg

Old Town

Step back in time as you wander through Nürnberg's charming Old Town, where medieval architecture meets vibrant modern culture.

Frankenjura

Discover the world-renowned climbing routes of the Frankenjura, a paradise for rock climbers nestled in the picturesque Bavarian landscape.

Surfen

Experience the thrill of city surfing at the Fuchsloch-Wave, a unique artificial wave in the heart of Nürnberg.
